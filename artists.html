Nachilima is a documentary photographer based in Livingstone, Zambia. Her work centres on the ordinary beauty of Southern Zambian life — people, animals, homes, skies, and the quiet spaces in between.

She holds a Bachelor of Commerce (Hons) from Delhi University (graduated 2022) and returned home to pursue photography full-time. She has worked as a freelance photographer and social media manager at Focus Africa Consulting, and completed a photography internship at UpTrend Gift Shop.

Nachilima is currently seeking her first solo exhibition. Her objective: to showcase work that finds the extraordinary in the everyday.

“I photograph the things I'm afraid I'll forget.”
